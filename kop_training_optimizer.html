Kingdom of Power · Pre-Event Planning

Troop Training Optimizer

This tool shows which troop tier(s) to train in the days before the event so that, once promoted to T10 with your available speedups, you earn the most KoP points. Training a low tier stockpiles more troops than you can afford to promote; training too high leaves speedups unused. Method 1 finds the single best tier; Method 2 tests whether splitting your pre-event days between T9 and a second tier is feasible.

Method 2 — T9 + Secondary Tier Split

Train T9 first (so you get some use out of them before the event), then switch all 3 queues to whichever other tier makes best use of your remaining days and remaining speedups. T9 gets first claim on the speedup budget — the secondary tier only gets what's left over.

Remaining days automatically go to the secondary tier.
